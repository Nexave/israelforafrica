About Israel4Africa

With a growing economy and a vast array of natural resources, Africa offers huge potential for Israeli businesses. Israel4Africa provides a platform for Israeli companies and other entities to access a large variety of tenders, calls for proposals and funding opportunities in various industries, including agriculture, water treatment, information & communication technology and more.
The Israel4Africa platform was launched by the Israeli Interest and Awareness in Africa Association (I2A3), an organization that aims to promote cooperation between Israel and African countries.

It is a subsidiary of Agora and Interteam, who specialize in initiating, developing, and managing large publicly funded multidisciplinary and multinational projects, providing a blend of strong management skills, extensive understanding of international partnerships, in-depth knowledge of large international programmes and above all, the skills to present your ideas and interests successfully in a competitive environment.
Israel4Africa aims to serve as tool for helping Israeli entities take part in the immense opportunities that the African continent offers. If you have identified an opportunity on our platform, which you would wish to explore in detail, need assistance in the submission process, or looking for partners or funding contact us.

I2A3 The Israeli Interest and Awareness in Africa Association I2A3 main goals are to improve trade relations, find opportunities and implement our extensive network of stakeholders to build a team of experts to provide the solutions across the entire Israeli ecosystem and to cooperate with local African partners.

The organization specialises in identifying African needs, find opportunities, and offer a platform for joint collaboration. I2A3 will provides detailed and customized information about the many opportunities that the African continent offers to Israel and coordinate the activities of the various stakeholders.

If you are looking for projects in specific fields or if you wish to concentrate on a specific country, search our database using key words.

Getting started

To get started, simply create an account and browse through our list of available tenders and call for proposals. You can filter by industry and country to find the opportunities that best suit your company's expertise. Once you've found either a tender, call for proposals or funding opportunity of interest, you can download the relevant documents from our easy-to-use online platform. Our team is available to answer any questions you may have and provide further assistance throughout the bidding process.
